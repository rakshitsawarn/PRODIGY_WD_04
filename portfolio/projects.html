Projects

  • E-commerce Platform Website and App: I've developed a comprehensive e-commerce platform that seamlessly integrates both a website and a mobile application. This platform is designed to provide users with a convenient and intuitive shopping experience, featuring robust product listings, secure payment gateways, and personalized recommendations. Whether users are browsing on their desktop or on the go, they can easily explore a wide range of products and make purchases with confidence.
  • Reporting Website for School Faculties: I've created a sophisticated reporting website tailored specifically for school faculties. This website enables educators to generate detailed reports on student performance, attendance, and behavior, providing valuable insights for academic planning and decision-making. With user-friendly interfaces and powerful analytics tools, this platform empowers faculties to track student progress, identify areas for improvement, and enhance the overall learning experience.
  • Weather Forecasting Mobile Application: I've developed a cutting-edge mobile application for weather forecasting that delivers accurate and up-to-date information to users in real-time. This application leverages advanced data analytics and predictive modeling techniques to generate precise weather forecasts for locations worldwide. With intuitive navigation and customizable settings, users can easily access weather updates, plan outdoor activities, and stay informed about changing weather conditions wherever they are.
  • QR Scanner Application: I've designed and implemented a QR scanner application that allows users to scan QR codes quickly and efficiently using their mobile devices. This application is equipped with advanced scanning algorithms and seamless integration with device cameras, ensuring fast and reliable QR code recognition. Whether users are accessing digital content, making payments, or checking in at events, this application provides a convenient and secure way to interact with QR code technology.
  • Fake Product Detection Application: I've developed an innovative application for detecting counterfeit products, helping consumers make informed purchasing decisions and combatting the proliferation of fake goods in the market. This application employs state-of-the-art image recognition algorithms and machine learning techniques to analyze product images and identify potential counterfeit items. By leveraging the power of technology, this application empowers users to protect themselves from fraudulent products and uphold product authenticity.
